About

Hi! I’m Ryan. I make comics, images, and posts about things I’ve interested in. One of my favourite challenges is taking a very complex topic, and trying to make it simple, accessible, and visually appealing. I make a lot of these images simply because nobody else has yet. I hate it when I’m trying to learn about a topic, but I can’t find any nice simple images, graphics or maps that summarise the information, and so I make my own.
